U Cut Carry Bag Punching Machine

U-cut carry bag punching machine provides a unique solution for u-cut of vest type bags (t-shirt bags) by its hydraulic punching machine. This bag punching machine provides premium class quality outputs with easy handling and almost zero maintenance cost. The bag punching machine’s pressure system is adjustable, and it produces a heavy-duty stroke for smooth cutting results of non-woven bags. The hydraulic bag punching machine for the non-woven bag is highly efficient, which ensures good quality output.


Technology-Driven

In this w-cut, carry bag punching machine bundle of bags coming from the non-woven bag making machine is taken and placed on the acrylic table tray on this machine; then this bundle of the non-woven bag is guided by adjusters on the table to attain u-cut, or w cut punch on the desired area of the bag. After that button is pressed and pre-fitted die on, the machine moves down to punch the bags. The waste is then removed, and the w cut non-woven bag or u-cut non-woven bag according to die shape is ready for packing and dispatch.


Applications

This non-woven u cut carry bag punching machine is very useful for the non-woven packing industry as it can do various jobs as following just by changing the shape of the die used in it:-


  • U cut bag
    •  
  • Any shape non-woven sheets
  • Also used for punching poly bags
  • D cut bag
