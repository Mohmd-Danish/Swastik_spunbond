ABOUT US

Swastik Spunbond Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company. We deal in the manufacturing and supply of non-woven processing machines all over India with the ambition of expanding our reach throughout the world.

Pp Woven bag making machines, ultrasonic loop handle machine, non-woven bag-making machines and EPE foam net-making machines are some of the finest machinery we have given to the country.

We have made it possible with strict quality control at every stage of the manufacturing process. Our focus also stays on doing extensive quality tests on the product. We use the finest technology available at our lab to conduct these tests. Moreover, it happens under the supervision of experienced engineers and technicians. It has helped us to face challenges fearlessly and grow faster.

Growth and Evolution

In our quest for technological innovations, we introduced to the country - water storage tanks that are highly durable and cost-effective. We are now India's leading and one of the fastest-growing water storage tanks solution provider company and a selected SKY WATER TANK in the water storage tanks category.

Innovation

Sky Industrial Containers has always believed in constant innovation to keep up with the changing society and its lifestyle, it was the first to introduce to the country blow mould water storage tanks, which are highly durable and cost-effective, not only this it is the first who has not only bring the colour concept in the tanks but also has come up with the attractive Granite finish layers in the tanks.
