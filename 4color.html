Four Color Bag Printing Machine

4 colors non woven offset printing machine is based on the CMYK color combination module and is well equipped with new generation technologies. This 4 color bag-to-bag printing machine is very versatile. It can print paper and non-woven bags, both with high-quality printing outputs, which gives unbelievable results in such an economical range.


These machines have an automatic feeder with a unique feeding system developed by our R&D team, automatic printing controlled by encoder and sensor in touch model, special common blanket technology for 2 color printing on common blanket, gripper chain delivery to deliver material properly, etc.


Applications

  • D cut bag
  • shoe bag
  • Sheets of non-woven
  • >U-cut/ W-cut bags
  • Side gusset non-woven bag
  • Creasing box bag
  • 3D Box bag
  • Laminated Non-Woven cut sheets
  • non woven pouches
