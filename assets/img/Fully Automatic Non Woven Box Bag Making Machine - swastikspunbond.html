Product Description

fully Automatic Non Woven box Bag Making Machine
The fully automatic non wovenbox bag making machine is quite popular in the paper bag
market. This machine has been designed with modern technology to produce abundant paper
bags.

No Wastage Of Resources
The non-woven bag making machine ensures the precise utilization of all resources and raw
materials to eliminate wastage and deliver the best quality.The non-woven bags machine never
lets you compromise on quality and perform automatically, thus helping you save on labor
expenses and put money back in your pocket.

Technology-Driven
With this machine, you can reduce your overall non woven bags project cost and meet
deadlines. It is equipped with an advanced plc-controlled touch screen panel that helps you
manage its diverse parameters single-handedly.The non-woven carry bag making machine
manufactures a range of bags based on size, color, design, and shape to meet all customers’
demands.The non-woven bag machines are made under our technical experts’ supervision, they
ensure that the machines are error-free, capable of delivering excellent performance, and can
serve the users for years.By ensuring the machine’s technical and physical attributes, we ensure
that they serve your production unit’s needs for a long time.
