Non Woven Loop Handle Bag Making Machine

This machine is to put the handle on non-woven flat bags, controlled by computer. It can automatically make a beautiful flower belt from 2 rolls of sheet materials and then transport the belts to the right place to complete handle bags. The efficiency is much higher than the manual machine. It’s very popular with clients both at home and abroad.


Swastik is one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers all over India. It delivers high-quality machine that produces a wide variety of handle bags. We help to operate your machine after purchasing and give full support in terms of warranty & training.


Applications

  • Handle loop non-woven bag
  • Bottom gusset handle loop bag
  • Side gusset handle loop bag

Salient Features

  • Superior Technology Control Panel with Touch Screen
  • Easily Adjustable Length of the Handle Loop
  • Durable, reliable, and safe
  • High-quality ultrasonic system
  • Smooth finishing and punch design
  • Fast production speen and stable performance
